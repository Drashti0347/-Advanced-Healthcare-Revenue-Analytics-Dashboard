# 🏥 Advanced Healthcare & Revenue Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Healthcare organizations generate massive volumes of patient, appointment, diagnosis, treatment, billing, and insurance data every day. Traditional reporting methods often lack interactivity and analytical depth, making it difficult for hospital administrators and healthcare professionals to monitor operational efficiency, patient care, and financial performance.

This project presents an interactive **Healthcare & Revenue Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** that transforms raw healthcare data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor patient demographics, clinical performance, treatment trends, insurance coverage, and revenue generation through dynamic and interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Monitor overall hospital revenue and operational performance.

✔ Analyze patient demographics and healthcare utilization.

✔ Track appointments, diagnoses, and treatment trends.

✔ Evaluate insurance coverage and billing performance.

✔ Identify key healthcare patterns to support strategic decision-making.

✔ Provide an interactive reporting solution for hospital administrators and management.

# 💡 Key Insights

📌 Adults contributed the highest proportion of hospital visits and overall revenue.

📌 Insurance-covered patients accounted for a significant share of total hospital revenue.

📌 Certain diagnosis categories recorded consistently higher patient volumes, indicating increased healthcare demand.

📌 Monthly revenue trends highlighted seasonal fluctuations in hospital income and patient activity.

📌 Patient demographics such as age group and gender revealed valuable insights into healthcare utilization patterns.

📌 Top revenue-generating patients contributed significantly to overall hospital revenue.
